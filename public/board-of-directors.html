Board of Directors

Arch. Mazherul Quader

Chairman

Arch. Mazherul Quader is a prominent entrepreneur and success driven figure in business community of Bangladesh with a diversified business portfolio. An eminent business personality who has been involved with over 330 projects at home and abroad. He has been entrusted to design many Commercial & Residential buildings in UAE, Liberia, Sri Lanka, Uganda & other countries. 35 years of rich and diversified experience in different business sectors such as Building & Infrastructure, Consultancy & Construction, Real Estate Developments, Agro, Ceramics etc. He has many rewards and recognitions to his credit for his business talent and his associations with different charitable organizations.

Mahin Bin Mazher

Managing Director

Philanthropic in mind, Dynamic in thinking, far-sighted in Vision; Mr. Mahin Mazher wields appreciably long experience in business that shaped up as a large conglomerate with the passage of time. Index group of companies is the epitome of his business leadership and corporate excellence, where he is the Managing Director. An accomplished business leader who is running diversified businesses and complex organizations in a wide range of industries including Agro, Ceramics, Investment & Hospitality, Real Estate & Construction, Distribution & Retail etc. An US graduate who served in many reputed North American Companies such as ‘Merrill Lynch’ and ‘Bell South Corporation’.

Md. Mamunur Rashid FCMA

Deputy Managing Director

Md. Mamunur Rashid FCMA, a Fellow Member of the Institute of Cost & Management Accountants of Bangladesh (ICMAB). Mr. Rashid has been working with Index Group since 2010, and currently serving as the Deputy Managing Director. He is also serving as the Independent Director of West Zone Power Distribution Company Ltd (WZPDCL), an Enterprise of Bangladesh Power Development Board and also serving as the Director of Nitol Insurance Co. Ltd. His career lifted off when he started as an officer of Bangladesh Steel & Engineering Corporation (BSEC) in the year 1989. After that he accumulated a wealth of experience via serving in the following influential designations and esteemed organizations: He served as Chief Financial Officer at Kazi Farms Group, Director (Finance) at Sheba Phone. He also served in Government organizations namely, Bangladesh Power Development Board (BPDB) and Bangladesh Parjatan Corporation (BPC).Mr. Rashid iscurrently serving as the Council member of the National Council of ICMAB. He also served as the Executive Secretary of South Asian Federation of Accountants (SAFA).
