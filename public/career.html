Why Should You Join X Index Companies?

Background of the
Organization

“X Index Companies”, established in the year 1977, has been a leading entity in various fields of business for over 4 decades. A market leader in the Ceramic Tiles industry, Index has successfully ventured into various sectors of the business arena. XICs history is deeply rooted as one of the first Architectural consultancy firms of free Bangladesh. XIC has substantial presence in Real Estate Construction, Hotel & Hospitality, Power Generation, Poultry Breeding, hatchery and Animal Feed, Ceramic Industry, Investment, and Media. The continued success of XIC is credited to the undying dedication of the owners, professional management and staff. The group of companies draw strength from collective efforts of employees backed by the companies’ policies and service rules that uphold our business ethics and organizational culture.

Working Environment

A trendsetter in every sen se, the management of X Index Companies promotes teamwork, creativity, individuality, transparency, and vibrancy in the office environment. The multi-talented management body is always keen to develop knowledge-base and skills in the employees. We proudly proclaim ‘work is fun’!

Career Progression

The work-environment and working opportunities that exist in X Index Companies empowered by corporate culture will ignite your passion, which in turn will help you to grow along with the organizational growth. The multi talented management body is always positive to develop your knowledge and skills to enable your growth in the career path aligning with the business.

Employee-Friendly Policy

We have competitive pay scale, 2 festival bonuses and PF, Gratuity, Earn leave facilities, annual and periodic performance review and increment, Group Insurance, critical illness coverage and all other standard practices that are expected in a top class organization.

The group employs 4000+ dynamic, energetic people and professionals to operate manufacturing, service, and technology-oriented businesses across the country and abroad. We have accordingly developed our HR policies and procedures as a recipe for long term association of the employees that starts from fair and impartial recruitment and selection, to development and retention processes.
