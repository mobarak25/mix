‘X Index’ Companies (XIC) is a business conglomerate with notable presence in Bangladesh. Initiating in the year 1977, with a heritage spanning over four decades, XIC established itself as a dynamic and progressive entity in the rapidly growing economy of Bangladesh.

Combining deep ethical values with proven business performance, the company built a heritage of deep social commitment that has earned the trust and respect of its customers. Five core values – Integrity, Ethics, Excellence, Unity and Responsibility – woven into the fabric of ‘X-Index’ and its brands are fundamental to its success. The name itself is a unique asset, representing ‘Leadership Through Experience’. It stands for consistent business excellence and profitable growth, with a commitment to the communities it serves and adherence to high standards of corporate governance.
